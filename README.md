# 🏠 Home Science Kenya - Senior School Learning App

**A free, offline-first Progressive Web App (PWA)** designed specifically for **Kenyan Senior Secondary School students** (Form 3 and Form 4) studying **Home Science** for the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE).

### Key Features
- ✅ **Completely Offline** – Works without internet after first use
- ✅ **Installable on Android** – Installs like a native app from the browser
- ✅ **KCSE Syllabus Aligned** – Covers all major topics from the official KICD curriculum
- ✅ **Interactive Quizzes** – KCSE-style questions with instant feedback
- ✅ **Practical Guides** – Step-by-step recipes, sewing techniques, and home care using local Kenyan resources
- ✅ **Progress Tracker** – Monitor your learning journey
- ✅ **Mobile-First Design** – Fast, clean, and easy to use on any smartphone

### Topics Covered
**Form 3**
- Meal Planning and Management
- Food Preparation and Cookery
- Laundry Work
- Clothing Construction (Basics)

**Form 4**
- Care of the Sick at Home
- Food Preservation and Convenience Foods
- Consumer Education and Protection
- Home Management (Ventilation, Lighting, Fuels, Soft Furnishings)
- Advanced Clothing Construction

### How to Use
1. Visit the app link: `https://yourusername.github.io/home-science-ke`
2. Tap the browser menu (⋮) → **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**
3. Start learning anytime, anywhere — even offline!

### Target Users
- Form 3 & Form 4 students preparing for KCSE
- Home Science teachers
- Parents supporting their children’s education
